With Mojo Helpdesk's new Chrome launcher, go directly from your Chrome home page to Mojo Helpdesk in just one click. It's simple to install, go to the [Chrome Web Store](https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/www.mojohelpdesk.com/blog-helpdesk-launch-pad/jgdhfkpnfbcanpgmakmgokcajpflgflj?hl=en-US&utm_source=chrome-ntp-launcher), find Mojo Helpdesk and click 'Launch Application'.
